Who We Are

Built to bridge a real gap

The data talent market is fragmented. Companies need experienced specialists quickly, but traditional staffing is too slow and generic. Freelance platforms lack structure and vetting. Consulting firms are expensive and inflexible.

Semantic was built to sit precisely in that gap. We curate a network of experienced data professionals, match them to company needs with precision, and stay involved throughout the engagement — ensuring things go well, not just get started.

Our core talent hub is based in Beirut — a city with a deep pool of technically strong, internationally experienced data professionals. We work closely with this community to maintain high standards and provide a structured, collaborative environment for growth.
